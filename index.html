About the test

This test uses logical and numerical reasoning to assess your potential IQ. Only basic math knowledge is required. The four basic operations (addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division) are sufficient.

The test's ability to predict an individual's IQ with high accuracy has a limit of 3.333 standard deviations, i.e., 150 (sd = 15), relative to the population average of 100. If you answer no questions correctly, your estimated IQ will be given as < 100 (lower than 100).
